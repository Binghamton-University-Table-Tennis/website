Binghamton University Table Tennis Club

Welcome!

The Table Tennis Club is designed to provide students with the opportunity to compete against other players as well as the opportunity to enhance their table tennis skills. Due to it's nature of activity and played inside and requires moderately little equipment, it's an ideal recreational activity for everyone. Although the club will be primarily recreational, players with competitive intent are welcome to join as well.

New Rating System

We now have a competitive rating system based on the USATT rating system for those that want to compare themselves to their fellow members. In each of the weekly practices, members will be able to play a ranked match to earn points.

Competitions

Yes! We will attend table tennis competitions! Each semester, we hope to send some club members to attend a tournament at RIT. We also plan to host a campus-wide tournament each semester. There will be more details as the events get organized.
