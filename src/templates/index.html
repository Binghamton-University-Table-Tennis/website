Binghamton University Table Tennis Club

Welcome!

The Table Tennis Club is designed to provide students with the opportunity to compete against other players as well as the opportunity to enhance their table tennis skills. Due to it's nature of activity and played inside and requires moderately little equipment, it's an ideal recreational activity for everyone. Although the club will be primarily recreational, players with competitive intent are welcome to join as well.

New Ladder System

We now have a new competitive ladder for those that want to compare themselves to their fellow members. In each of the weekly practices, members will be able to play a ranked match to earn points.

Competitions

Yes we will have table tennis tournaments! Each semester we hope to have some members that are interested attend a tournament at RIT. We do also plan to host a campus tournament at some point this school year. There will be an announcement when it gets organized.
