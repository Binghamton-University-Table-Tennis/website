Welcome!

The Table Tennis Club is designed to provide students with the opportunity to compete against other players as well as the opportunity to enhance their table tennis skills. Requiring minimal equipment, it is an ideal recreational activity for everyone. Although the club will be primarily recreational, players with competitive intent are welcome to join as well.

Rating System

We now have a competitive rating system based on the USATT rating system for those that want to compare themselves to their fellow members. In each of the weekly practices, members will be able to play a ranked match to earn points. View the rating system

Competitions

Yes! We will attend table tennis competitions! Each semester, we hope to send some club members to attend the NCTTA Upper New York Central Divisional Tournaments and beyond. We also plan to host a campus-wide tournament each semester. View previous tournament photos
